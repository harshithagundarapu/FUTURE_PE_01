AI Website Copy Generator for Local Businesses
Future Interns – Prompt Engineering Task 1 (2026)
Project Overview

This project was developed as part of the Future Interns Prompt Engineering Task 1 (2026). The objective of the assignment is to demonstrate how Prompt Engineering and Artificial Intelligence can be used to generate high-quality, conversion-focused website copy for local businesses.

Many small businesses provide excellent products and services but struggle to communicate their value through their websites. Their homepage may be unclear, service descriptions may be too generic, and call-to-action sections often fail to encourage visitors to take action. Professional copywriting services are expensive, making it difficult for small business owners to improve their online presence.

This project addresses that problem by designing a reusable prompt framework that produces professional website copy within minutes. Instead of manually writing every section of a website, users can provide business information and receive well-structured, engaging, and persuasive content suitable for immediate publication.

For this project, a local salon named ABC Salon, Bangalore has been selected as the sample client. The prompt framework has been customized to generate homepage content, service descriptions, trust-building elements, and strong call-to-action sections specifically for the beauty industry. However, the same framework can easily be adapted for cafés, coaching institutes, clinics, agencies, restaurants, freelancers, and many other local businesses.

Objectives

The project focuses on achieving the following objectives:

Design reusable prompts for AI content generation.
Generate a compelling homepage with a clear value proposition.
Create professional service page descriptions.
Produce persuasive CTA sections.
Support multiple writing tones such as friendly, professional, and confident.
Deliver content that is ready to publish on a real business website.
